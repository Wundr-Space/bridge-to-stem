## Page 6: Market Opportunity

### What to Include:

**TAM / SAM / SOM approach:**

**Total Addressable Market (TAM):**
- Size of broader market
- Example: "Tech career training = ¬£20B globally"

**Serviceable Available Market (SAM):**
- Reachable portion of TAM
- Example: "Gen Z jobseekers in UK"

**Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM):**
- Early adopter niche you can realistically capture
- Example: "10k bootcamp-ready users via TikTok ads"

## üß† Final Notes

### Key Principles:

1. **Frame as a venture, not a project**
   - Show commercial viability alongside social impact
   - Demonstrate scalability and growth potential
   - Position as investment opportunity

2. **Lead with problem clarity, close with scalable impact + viability**
   - Hook audience with compelling problem
   - Build case through solution and traction
   - Close with vision and concrete next steps

3. **Use real user insights as much as possible**
   - Quote actual users/customers
   - Reference specific pain points from research
   - Show evidence of demand

4. **Keep slides visual, not text-heavy**
   - 1 key image/chart per slide
   - Minimal bullet points (3-5 max)
   - Use data visualizations
   - Include photos of users/team where relevant

5. **Anticipate follow-up questions:**
   - **Traction:** What evidence of demand exists?
   - **Defensibility:** What prevents competitors from copying?
   - **Pricing:** How did you arrive at pricing? Will customers pay?
   - **Ethics:** How do you handle data privacy, safeguarding?
   - **Scalability:** What are the bottlenecks to growth?
   - **Unit economics:** What does customer acquisition cost vs. lifetime value?

## Pitch Deck Checklist

Before finalizing, ensure you can answer:

- [ ] **Problem:** Is the pain point clear and urgent?
- [ ] **Solution:** Can a non-expert understand what you built?
- [ ] **Market:** Are market size estimates grounded in reality?
- [ ] **Business Model:** Is it clear how you make money?
- [ ] **Differentiation:** What makes you 10x better, not 10% better?
- [ ] **Team:** Why is this team uniquely positioned to win?
- [ ] **Traction:** What evidence shows this is working?
- [ ] **Ask:** Is it crystal clear what you need and why?
- [ ] **Impact:** How does this create meaningful social change at scale?
- [ ] **Visuals:** Is every slide visual-first, not text-heavy?
